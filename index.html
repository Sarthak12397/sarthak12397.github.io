Project 01 · FinancialLedgerEngine

Financial Ledger Engine

Double-entry bookkeeping · Append-only audit trail

An immutable double-entry ledger with domain-enforced balance invariants. No UPDATE ever. Every money movement is an append — full audit trail preserved permanently under all conditions including retries, partial failures, and concurrent writes.

  • Double-entry invariant: total debits = total credits enforced at domain layer
  • Append-only architecture — no UPDATE, immutable record from day one
  • Idempotency layer preventing duplicate money movement under retries
  • UnitOfWork atomic boundary — partial writes physically impossible
  • BalanceVerificationJob detecting drift between stored and computed values
  • 7 xUnit tests proving mathematical correctness
